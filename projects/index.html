XLSForm Quality Reviewer
Tool

A quality assurance tool for household survey instruments. Upload an XLSForm and get a structured report covering structural issues, simulation-based logic errors, and survey design recommendations — before the form reaches the field.

  • Structural inspection: broken groups, duplicate names, missing metadata
  • Simulation across hundreds of synthetic respondents to expose unreachable questions
  • Design suggestions across 13 categories with ready-to-copy fix expressions
  • Exportable HTML report with severity badges

Survey HFC Dashboard
Tool

A high-frequency data quality monitoring dashboard for food security and household surveys. Upload daily data to track enumerator behavior, flag rates, duplicate records, and cross-indicator contradictions.

  • Ten indicator modules: FCS, rCSI, HDDS, LCS, expenditure, timing, and more
  • FEWS NET cross-indicator illogicality checks
  • Enumerator monitoring: flag rates, means, daily submission heatmaps
  • Survey status, progress-to-target, and priority action items
